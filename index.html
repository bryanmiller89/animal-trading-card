Red Fox

Red foxes have excellent hearing. Red foxes can reportedly hear a watch ticking 40 yards away!

  • Average Lifespan: 2 - 5 years
  • Top Speed: 31 miles per hour
  • Average Mass: 4.9 - 31 pounds
  • Average Height: 14 - 20 inches

Red foxes are small-to-medium-sized, omnivorous mammals belonging to several genera of the family Canidae. Red foxes have a flattened skull, upright triangular ears, a pointed, slightly upturned snout, and a long bushy tail.
